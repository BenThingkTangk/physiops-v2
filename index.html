04 — Anatomy of the Signal

Where the autonomic nervous system speaks.

The vagus nerve isn't a metaphor. It's the longest cranial nerve, the parasympathetic superhighway, and our primary diagnostic substrate. Sympathetic outflow runs the chain on the other side. We measure both.

P

Vagus · Cranial Nerve X

Parasympathetic outflow to heart, lungs, gut. Measured via high-frequency HRV (RFa) and beat-to-beat respiratory sinus arrhythmia.

S

Sympathetic Chain

Bilateral ganglia from C7 to L2. Decoded via low-frequency power (LFa) and time-domain RMSSD inversion against baseline.

H

Composite HRV

Violet line you'll see throughout the platform. The brain-body conversation, integrated into a single longitudinal signal.

14 — Root-cause medicine

A paradigm shift, drawn in a single line.

Modern medicine treats symptoms downstream of the autonomic nervous system. PhysioPS reads the upstream branch directly — the regulator, not the regulated.

DOWNSTREAM

Hypertension. Migraine. Anxiety. Chronic fatigue. IBS. Insomnia. Each one a chapter in a long book of symptoms — and each one a manifestation of the same autonomic dysregulation upstream.

UPSTREAM

The autonomic nervous system regulates every involuntary function. When it drifts, everything downstream drifts with it. Read the regulator and you read the system.

18 — Origin

Steve O'Leary, and the catalyst event.

The platform exists because a single patient — a Navy SEAL community member with post-deployment dysautonomia — couldn't get a clean autonomic readout in any of three academic medical centers. We built the readout.

FOUNDER · 2019

"He was alive. The system couldn't see him."

Steve O'Leary spent fifteen years building data infrastructure for biopharma. In 2019, a friend in the SEAL community tried to get a vagal-tone reading and walked out of three institutions with three different non-answers. PhysioPS started as a single-page tool to aggregate the readings he could get. It became the platform when the same pattern showed up in Long COVID, in PTSD, in cardiac rehab, and in pediatric POTS — five conditions, one missing instrument.
